## Consultation Meeting Guide

### Pre-Meeting Preparation:
- Review student's current grades
- Prepare subject-specific diagnostic questions
- Bring sample materials
- Have calendar ready for scheduling

### Meeting Structure (30 minutes):

**Introduction (5 minutes):**
```
"Thank you for taking the time to meet with me. I'm Ahmed Waqar, and I've been tutoring GCSE students for 12 years. Today, I'd like to:
‚Ä¢ Understand [Student Name]'s current situation
```
